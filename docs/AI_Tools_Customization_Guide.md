# AI Tools Customization Guide

This guide provides a comprehensive overview and practical steps to customize popular AI tools like Gemini, Google AI Studio, Notebook LLM, MCP, and n8n. It covers setup, integration, training, workflow automation, and optimization.

---

## 1. Gemini Customization

### Setup and Access
* **Create an account** and obtain API credentials.
* **Configure workspace** and project settings.

### Integration
* **Connect Gemini APIs** with your data sources.
* **Use SDKs or REST APIs** for custom workflows.

### Training and Fine-tuning
* **Upload datasets** for model fine-tuning.
* **Monitor training progress** and adjust parameters.

### Workflow Automation
* **Build pipelines** using Gemini's workflow builder.
* **Set triggers and actions** for automation.

### Optimization
* **Analyze model performance** metrics.
* **Implement feedback loops** for continuous improvement.

---

## 2. Google AI Studio Customization

### Project Setup
* **Create a new project** in Google Cloud Console.
* **Enable AI Studio APIs** and services.

### Data Preparation
* **Import and preprocess** datasets.
* **Use AutoML** or custom training options.

### Model Training
* **Configure training jobs** with desired parameters.
* **Monitor and evaluate** model accuracy.

### Deployment
* **Deploy models** to endpoints.
* **Set up monitoring** and logging.

### Integration
* **Use REST APIs** or client libraries to integrate models.

## 3. Notebook LLM Customization

### Environment Setup
* **Install necessary libraries** and dependencies.
* **Configure Jupyter** or your preferred notebook environment.

### Model Loading
* **Load pre-trained** LLM models.
* **Customize tokenizer** and model parameters.

### Fine-tuning
* **Prepare training data**.
* **Run fine-tuning scripts** with appropriate hyperparameters.

### Inference and Testing
* **Test model outputs**.
* **Adjust prompts and parameters** for desired results.

### Automation
* **Integrate notebooks** with workflow tools for automation.

Customizing AI tools involves a combination of setup, integration, training, automation, and continuous optimization. Using visual workflow builders and SDKs can streamline these processes, making your AI tools more effective and tailored to your specific enterprise needs in Shetue OS.
